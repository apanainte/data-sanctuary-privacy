Privacy Policy for Data Sanctuary

Effective Date: July 4, 2025
Last Updated: July 4, 2025

Overview

Data Sanctuary is a document management app designed with privacy as our top priority. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data. Everything stays on your device.

Our Privacy Promise

✅ Your photos never leave your device

✅ No data collection or tracking

✅ No third-party analytics

✅ No user accounts or cloud storage

✅ No advertising or data sharing

What Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or have access to:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Photo content or metadata
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or app behavior data
  • Crash reports or diagnostic information
  • Any other personal or sensitive information

How Your Photos Are Stored

Local Storage Only

  • All photos are stored directly in your device's secure storage
  • Photos are organized in categories you choose (Health, Financial, Personal, Insurance, Other)
  • Category information is stored as small text files on your device
  • No photos or data are uploaded to our servers or any cloud service

Your Control

  • You have complete control over your photos
  • Delete the app to remove all stored photos
  • Use iTunes/Finder backup to include photos in device backups
  • Share photos using your device's built-in sharing features

Permissions We Request

Camera Access

  • Why we need it: To capture photos of your documents
  • What we do with it: Take photos and save them locally on your device
  • What we don't do: We don't access your existing photo library, send photos anywhere, or use the camera for any other purpose
Permission Text: "This app needs camera access to capture photos of documents for storage and organization."

Data Security

Since all data stays on your device:

  • Your photos are protected by your device's built-in security (Face ID, Touch ID, passcode)
  • We cannot access your photos remotely
  • No risk of data breaches from our servers (we don't have any!)
  • Your data is as secure as your device

Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party services that could access your data, including:

  • Analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • Crash reporting tools
  • Advertising networks
  • Cloud storage providers
  • Social media integrations

Children's Privacy

Data Sanctuary is safe for users of all ages because:

  • We don't collect any personal information
  • No online features or communication
  • No user accounts or profiles
  • Parents have full control through device settings

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our privacy practices (though we don't plan to), we will:

  • Update this policy with a new "Last Updated" date
  • Notify users through an app update
  • Continue to prioritize your privacy

International Users

Since we don't collect data, international privacy laws like GDPR and CCPA don't apply to our data handling. However, we support the principles of these laws:

  • Right to know: This policy explains our practices
  • Right to delete: Delete the app to remove all data
  • Right to control: You control all data on your device

Technical Details

For transparency, here's exactly what happens with your data:

When You Take a Photo:

1. Camera captures image
2. You select a category
3. Photo saved to device storage as JPEG file
4. Category metadata saved as JSON file
5. Nothing sent to internet

File Locations:

  • Photos: Device Documents directory
  • Categories: Local JSON files
  • App settings: Device preferences only

The short version: Data Sanctuary is a completely private photo storage app. Your photos stay on your device, we don't collect any data, and we can't see or access anything you store. It's designed to be as private as physically possible while still being useful.
